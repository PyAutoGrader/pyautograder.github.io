## What You Need

- Login credentials from your instructor
- The student client installed (see [Student Downloads](/student-downloads/))

## Overview of Steps

1. Download the student client
2. Install it on your machine
3. Open the client and log in with credentials from your instructor
4. Select your assignment
5. Point the client at your Python file
6. Submit and view your results
