PyAutoGrader is completely free and always will be. No subscriptions, no per-student fees.

How It Works

PyAutoGrader has two applications - one for instructors and one for students. The instructor creates an assignment file (.agr) with tests, solutions, and configuration. Students load the .agr file into their app, run the grader on their code, and get instant feedback. Results can be collected via PDF export, email, Google Sheets, or a self-hosted server.

Instructor Workflow

1

Download the Instructor Executable

Download the instructor executable for your operating system from this page.

2

Create an Assignment File

Open the instructor app and create a new assignment. Add your test cases and configure grading criteria. Save the assignment as a .agr file.

3

Share the Assignment File

Distribute the .agr file to your students via your LMS, email, or any file sharing method. Students download the student executable separately from the Student Downloads page.

4

Collect Results

Students export a PDF of their grading results and submit it to your LMS or send it to you directly.
