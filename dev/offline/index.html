Offline Autograder

Automated Python grading with no server required.

The Offline Autograder is completely free and always will be.

How It Works

The Offline Autograder consists of two executables ‚Äî one for instructors and one for students. Both are downloaded directly from this site. The instructor creates an assignment file (.agr) which the student loads into their executable. After completing the assignment, the student exports a results PDF and submits it to the LMS or sends it to their instructor.

Instructor Download

Download the instructor executable to create and manage assignment files (.agr). Your students will download their own executable separately from the Student Downloads page.

Instructor Workflow

1

Download the Instructor Executable

Download the instructor executable for your operating system from this page.

2

Create an Assignment File

Open the instructor app and create a new assignment. Add your test cases and configure grading criteria. Save the assignment as a .agr file.

3

Share the Assignment File

Distribute the .agr file to your students via your LMS, email, or any file sharing method. Students download the student executable separately from the Student Downloads page.

4

Collect Results

Students export a PDF of their grading results and submit it to your LMS or send it to you directly.

Are You a Student?

If your instructor uses the Offline Autograder, head to the Student Downloads page to download your executable and get setup instructions.
