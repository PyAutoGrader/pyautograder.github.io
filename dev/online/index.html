How It Works

The Online Autograder is a hosted platform with separate clients for instructors and students. Instructors manage assignments and view results through the instructor client; students download the student client to submit their work.

Instructor Client

The instructor client is your command center for managing assignments, test cases, and viewing student results. Request an account to get started.

  • Create and manage grading assignments
  • Upload test cases and configure grading criteria
  • View student submission results in real time
  • Manage your class roster

Student Client

Students download the student client and use it to submit their Python assignments directly to the grading server. Results are returned immediately.

  • Submit assignments directly from the client
  • Receive grading feedback instantly
  • No account setup required for students — login provided by instructor
