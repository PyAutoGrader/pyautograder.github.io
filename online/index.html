Online Autograder

A hosted platform for managing and grading Python assignments.

Instructor accounts are available by request. Contact us to get access.

How It Works

The Online Autograder has two clients ‚Äî one for instructors and one for students ‚Äî both communicating with a hosted server. The server stores all assignment data, class rosters, and submission results. Grading itself runs locally on the student's machine, keeping things fast and reducing server load.

Instructor Workflow

1

Request an Account

Fill out the request form and you will receive instructor login credentials by email.

2

Download the Instructor Client

Download the instructor client for your operating system from this page.

3

Set Up Your Class

Log in and create your class. Add students directly or share a join code ‚Äî the server will email login credentials to each student automatically.

4

Create Assignments

Build assignments in the client, configure your grading criteria, and publish them to your class. Students will see new assignments in their client immediately.

5

View Results

As students submit, results appear in your instructor client in real time. Export grades when you are ready.

Are You a Student?

If your instructor uses the Online Autograder, download the student client from the Student Downloads page. Your instructor will provide a join code or add you directly ‚Äî you will receive your login credentials by email.
