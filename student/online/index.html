Getting Started

1

Get Added to Your Class

Your instructor will either add you directly or give you a join code. Once added, your login credentials will be sent to your email automatically.

2

Download the Client

Download the student client for your operating system using the button above.

3

Log In

Open the client and log in with the credentials from your email. Enter your instructor's server address if prompted.

4

Download and Complete Your Assignment

Your assignments will appear in the client. Download the assignment, complete your Python work, and submit through the client. Grading runs on your machine and results are sent back to the server automatically.
